About IqubLedger

Modern financial management for traditional Iqub groups

Secure • Transparent • Efficient

Our Mission

IqubLedger was created to bring modern financial technology to traditional rotating savings and credit associations (ROSCAs), known as Iqub in Ethiopia.

We believe that financial management should be secure, transparent, and accessible to everyone. Our platform digitizes the traditional Iqub system while preserving its cultural significance and community trust.

Bank-Grade Security

End-to-end encryption, two-factor authentication, and secure transactions ensure your financial data is protected.

Complete Transparency

Real-time transaction tracking, detailed reports, and audit trails ensure everyone can see exactly where funds are going.

Digital Efficiency

Automated calculations, instant notifications, and mobile access replace manual bookkeeping and reduce errors.

Community Focused

Designed with community trust at its core, supporting group dynamics while providing individual financial management.
